ABOUT US

Introducing a revolutionary health monitoring device aimed at assessing spinal health with unparalleled precision. Equipped with posture sensors affixed to the spine and force sensors integrated into shoes, this innovative system analyzes the alignment of the spine based on the wearer's shoe sole patterns. By wearing the sensor-equipped spine band and shoes, users can receive real-time insights into their spinal alignment, crucial for maintaining optimal posture and health. Generated reports are securely stored in the cloud, facilitating periodic progress updates and identification of potential issues. Developed with cutting-edge AI technology, this device not only provides accurate alignment assessments but also predicts and displays potential spinal problems arising from daily activities, ensuring proactive management of spinal health.

Spine Health

The health monitoring device tracks spine alignment using sensors on the spine and shoes, correlating data to assess posture. Reports are stored in the cloud with AI analysis, providing real-time feedback and alerts for potential problems. Periodical progress reports are generated to track improvements in spine health over time.

AI Analysis

Our groundbreaking spine health monitor combines advanced sensor technology with AI analysis for real-time posture feedback. With sensors on the spine and shoes, users get personalized reports stored securely in the cloud. It enables proactive spine health management with precision and convenience.

Pressure Analysis

Our device includes foot pressure analysis through embedded force sensors in the shoes, providing insights into gait, balance, and weight distribution. Combined with spine sensor data, it offers comprehensive posture monitoring for personalized recommendations and improved alignment.
